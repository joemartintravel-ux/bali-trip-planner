The Trip
3 Days, 2 Nights in Bali

We're getting a group of Nomad Circle members together for a few days in Bali. The plan is simple — stay in a quality villa, run some mastermind sessions, get to know each other properly, and make the most of what Bali has to offer. No fluff, no over-produced itinerary. Just a good group of people, good conversations, and a few days you'll remember.

◆
Mastermind Sessions
Focused sessions where you can share what you're working on, get feedback, and learn from the room.
◆
Networking
Meet other entrepreneurs and location-independent professionals in a relaxed, no-pressure setting.
◆
Activities
Padel, beach days, exploring Bali — plenty of downtime to enjoy the island between sessions.
◆
Dinners
Evening meals together at some of Bali's best spots. Great food, better company.
Designed to be affordable. This isn't a luxury retreat with a luxury price tag. We're splitting costs across the group — expect just a few hundred pounds depending on final numbers. The goal is to make it accessible for everyone.
